CHANGING THE PROCESS

We work closely together to change the process of making Mead at Yggdrassil..

Our goal: to develop the perfect final product to be used for making the best blends. Each blend is different in texture, flavor, color and, of course, aroma: making room for individuality.

That’s why each flavor requires a different preparation process, but always with the same attention and respect for tradition.
